Outdoor Activities

Running | Biking | Climbing

When not working, Greg is outdoors as much as possible. This includes being involved in almost any activity you can think of. Taking the time to enjoy nature allows for better concentration and the ability to work harder at school, while having an intellectually stimulating job also improves outdoor abilities.

Greg grew up playing many different sports but focused mainly on soccer. Since then, he has played soccer in men's leagues and has started playing Ultimate Frisbee to keep his competitive drive satisfied. Running is one of his greatest passions and in that pursuit he has run three marathons and paced friends at ultramarathons.
